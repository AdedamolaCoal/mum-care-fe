About MUM CARE

At MumCare, we believe every mother and child deserves access to quality healthcare. Our mission is to empower healthcare providers and families with tools to improve maternal and child health outcomes. Designed with hospitals in mind, MumCare simplifies the process of tracking antenatal visits, supplements, and child immunizations, ensuring no vital detail is overlooked. By combining technology with a deep understanding of maternal health, we aim to create a seamless experience for hospitals and mothers, fostering healthier communities and brighter futures.

Our Goal

Enhance Maternal Health

  • Empower mothers with tools to stay informed and adhere to antenatal care schedules, improving health outcomes.

Support Hospitals

  • Simplify hospital workflows by providing a centralized system for tracking and managing patient records.

Protect Future Generations

  • Ensure every child receives their immunizations on time by maintaining accurate and accessible vaccination records.
